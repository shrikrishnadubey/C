Write macros named GET_RED, GET_GREEN, and GET_BLUE that, when given a color as
an argument (see Exercise 4), return its 8-bit red, green, and blue
intensitities.

Answer:
#define GET_RED(color) ((color) & 0xff)
#define GET_GREEN(color) ((color) >> 8 & 0xff)
#define GET_BLUE(color) ((color) >> 16 & 0xff)
